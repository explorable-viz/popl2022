\subsection{\thmref{surface-language:desugar:gc}, part (ii)}
Induction on the $\dfwdarrow$ derivation.
\begin{proof}
   Suppose $e, s \dbwdarrow s'$. Then $\desugarBwdF{s}(\desugarFwdF{s}(s')) \leq s'$.
