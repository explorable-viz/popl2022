\subsection{\thmref{surface-language:desugar:gc}, part (i)}
Induction on the $\dbwdarrow$ derivation.
\begin{proof}
   Suppose $s \dfwdarrow e$. Then $\desugarFwdF{s}(\desugarBwdF{s}(e')) \geq e'$.
